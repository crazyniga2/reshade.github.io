What Are RTX Shaders?

RTX shaders replicate realistic lighting by simulating the physical behavior of light. Through effects like global illumination, ambient occlusion, reflections, and shadows, they dramatically enhance visual depth and realism. While Roblox does not natively support full RTX, our solution leverages advanced post-processing via ReShade to bring these visual enhancements to life.
